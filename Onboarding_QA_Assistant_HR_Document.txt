3. Company Policies
Code of Ethics and Conduct:
- We expect all employees to adhere to ethical guidelines and maintain professionalism in the workplace.
- Harassment of any kind is not tolerated.

Working Hours:
- Our standard working hours are from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday through Friday.
- Flexibility is available based on your role.

Leave Policy:
- Employees are entitled to 22 paid vacation days per year excluding sick leaves.
- 4 Sick leaves are available per year.

- Retirement Benefits: A 401(k) plan with company matching.

A1: You can submit your timesheet through the HR portal by the end of each week.

A2: Yes, employees can work from home for up to 3 days a week based on their manager's approval.
